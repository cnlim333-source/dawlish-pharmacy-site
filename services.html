What We Offer

At Dawlish Warren Pharmacy, we offer a wide range of NHS and private services tailored to support your health and wellbeing. Our services include:

  • NHS Pharmacy First Service – Quick and easy access to advice and treatment for common conditions without needing a GP appointment.
  • COVID-19 Vaccinations – Get vaccinated safely and efficiently at our pharmacy.
  • Blood Pressure Checks – Monitor and manage your blood pressure with our professional support.
  • Contraception Services – Access confidential and professional contraceptive advice and supply.
  • Travel Clinic – Prepare for your travels with vaccinations and advice tailored to your destination.
  • Weight Loss Support – Join our weight management programmes with personalised guidance and medication options.
  • Ear Wax Removal – Safe and effective ear wax removal by trained professionals.
